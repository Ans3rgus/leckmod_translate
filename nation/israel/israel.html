Israel - David

Ability: Promised Land

+1 Culture from Pastures.

Unit: Maccabee

Replaces Swordsmen. Earn 100% of foe's Strength as Faith from kills. Significantly cheaper to construct (40 hammers vs. 50).
Does not require Iron to construct.

Building: King Solomon's Temple

Replaces the National College. In addition to the typical perks, grants +3 faith and awards 50 science and 50 faith upon expending a great person. May not be built with production and instead has to be bought with faith (260) or gold (300) (on quick speed).

Bias: None
